Who Are We?

We are a Christ-Centered biblical 12 step program designed to help Everyone recover from negative behaviors and choices in life.

Life recovery recognizes the Bible as the source of authority given by God and applies His Word to empower people throught Jesus Christ to address human issues in a community of believers who provide support and encouragement to overcome. The twelve-steps of Life Recovery have been proven effective tools to overcome problems, and addictions of various types. We offer a safe community where people can recover from the hurts and pains of life.

Examples include: drugs, alcohol, sex addiction, gambling and food addictions, co-dependency and other life issues.
