Don't tell them Jesus Loves them...

until you are ready to love them too:

All these afflicted ones feel their life fade away, to face the end alone. We say a prayer for their needs, afraid to touch, to hurt, to bleed. Do you feel their pain, has it touched your life? Can you taste the salt in their tears they cry? Will you love them back to life again?...These are lives we can't ignore. Will you turn away? Will you see Jesus in each of them? There is hope and grace for them. They have not strayed too far. Will you love them back to life again? challenge by Steve Camp


There are several ways that you can partner with us.



  • Pray
    We covet your prayers! Pray specifically that our guests will notice God's hand at work from the moment they come through the door, till the moment they leave. Pray that our counselors will rightly divide the Word of God, and discern the needs of those who come.

  • Give
    We are a faith based ministry. All the clothes are donated. We can always use clothes, specifically children's clothes, toys, diapers, and ...

  • If you feel God wants you to give a financial donation, you can do so here Please specify from the dropdown menu the donation is for the care center.

  • serve
    Clothes usually come in a garbage bag. We have a dedicated staff of volunteers who sort, organize, clean, and hang the clothes, toys and foods we receive.

  • Worship with us
    We invite the community to come to the care center Sunday morning for a time of worship. Come at 11:00AM Sunday morning.
